## License Management

This section explains how to manage your On-Prem license after installation, including deassociating a license and adding new targets or credits.

***

### When Deassociation Is Required

You must deassociate your license if:

* You plan to move the On-Prem installation to another machine
* You need to reinstall On-Prem from scratch
* The current server is being retired or replaced

Deassociation ensures that the license can be safely reused.

***

### Deassociate License from On-Prem

1. Log in to your **ZeroThreat Cloud account**
2. Navigate to your organization’s license (![](https://1825008717-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2Fs6Y7hKb1RwZWFZo4EnUm%2Fuploads%2FeXFNxDl9cYytJrXdN7Yc%2Fimage.png?alt=media\&token=7da0b460-4efd-4b75-be3b-a5e48318de47)) section. This can be accessed organization settings.&#x20;
3. Click on **Deassociate Licenses** butto&#x6E;**.**

4. Copy the generated De-Association token.

<figure><img src="https://1825008717-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2Fs6Y7hKb1RwZWFZo4EnUm%2Fuploads%2F0xPnMAkM9UqWJ5WZxKUv%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=15e18092-8d5c-4a23-bda7-86d791a44d84" alt="" width="431"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5. Return to the On-Prem CLI:

<figure><img src="https://1825008717-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2Fs6Y7hKb1RwZWFZo4EnUm%2Fuploads%2FKlH5XprSyQNjUIeBTogG%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=e6009590-e9f3-4e3d-82a2-c038dc0e73ef" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

###### Failing to de-associate license before reinstalling on another machine will result in block/error during license activation process.
::

### Adding New Targets or Credits

To add targets or credits to your existing on-prem installation, you will need to purchase them from the Cloud version. Once done:

1. Log in to your **On-Prem ZeroThreat** URL
2. Navigate to your organization’s license (![](https://1825008717-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2Fs6Y7hKb1RwZWFZo4EnUm%2Fuploads%2FeXFNxDl9cYytJrXdN7Yc%2Fimage.png?alt=media\&token=7da0b460-4efd-4b75-be3b-a5e48318de47)) section. This can be accessed organization settings

<figure><img src="https://1825008717-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2Fs6Y7hKb1RwZWFZo4EnUm%2Fuploads%2FKHrrzY1UrECSMBEzRnPL%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=e86c7a27-55a1-497b-b9ac-1fba67469ec3" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. Click **Activate License** (<img src="https://1825008717-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2Fs6Y7hKb1RwZWFZo4EnUm%2Fuploads%2Fjh0YjzLrocRcV5Ei9jsF%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=ec82ac05-5b24-43d1-a7ca-850e0429408f" alt="" data-size="line">)
4. Enter the license key for the top-up. You will need to paste the license key generated in your licenses section in your **ZeroThreat Cloud account** organization.&#x20;

<figure><img src="https://1825008717-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2Fs6Y7hKb1RwZWFZo4EnUm%2Fuploads%2F5pHek6XK9541eFUX2ZFm%2Fimage.png?alt=media&#x26;token=2af63da1-9924-4246-9a91-a50d8e8929c2" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once Activated you will see a successful activation screen.\
\
The newly purchased capacity will be added in your On-Prem local installation.

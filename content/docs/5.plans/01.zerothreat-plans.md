## ZeroThreat Plans Overview

ZeroThreat offers multiple plans designed to support different security testing needs ‚Äî from individual practitioners and small teams to large enterprises and managed security providers.

Each plan provides a different level of scanning capacity, automation, integrations, reporting, and deployment flexibility. This page explains the available plans and their capabilities so that security teams and decision makers can choose the most appropriate option for their organization.

### Plan Categories

ZeroThreat plans are grouped by customer type:

* **Free** ‚Äì Long-term free tier with limited capabilities
* **Professional** ‚Äì For security teams running regular cloud or on-demand scans
* **Enterprise** ‚Äì For organizations and service providers requiring scale, automation, governance, and private deployment

### Free Plan

#### Best for: Individuals and small teams getting started with DAST

The Free Plan provides ongoing access to ZeroThreat with limited scanning and reporting capabilities. It is intended for evaluation, and lightweight testing, and does not provide access to paid or enterprise features.

**Included**

* 1 parallel scan per host
* 5 scans on signup (limited report format)
* 1 free scan credit added each month (limited report format)
* Partial Scan Report with limited visibility into found vulnerabilties

**Not included**

* Full comprehensive Scan Report&#x20;
* Re-testing
* CI/CD integrations
* Issue tracking
* Notification Integration
* Production-safe testing
* Agentic AI Pentesting
* Custom attack templates
* Advanced reporting and branding

> <span style="font-style: normal !important; display: inline-block; width: 100%;"> ‚ÑπÔ∏è **Limited Scan Report Format (Free Plan) :** </span>
> <p style="padding-left: 25px; font-style: normal">The Free Plan delivers scan reports in a restricted format that demonstrates the scanner's detection capabilities while limiting full access to findings. When a vulnerability is identified, the complete HTTP request and response details are shown only for the first occurrence. Additional instances of the same vulnerability are redacted and obscured. This same restriction extends to other security findings including outdated packages, SSL/certificate vulnerabilities, and cloud misconfigurations, which also appear partially hidden. In essence, this format proves the scanner can identify security issues in your application, but withholds comprehensive details across all findings which are reserved for premium plan subscribers.</p>

### Professional Plans

Professional plans are designed for security teams that require regular scanning with predictable billing or flexible, usage-based testing.

#### Professional Pay-Per-Scan

#### Best for: Teams that perform security testing occasionally or on demand

This plan uses a **credit-based scanning model** instead of a recurring subscription.

Security teams purchase scan credits in advance and use those credits to run scans as needed.

**How it works**

* Purchase scan credits
* Each scan consumes one credit
* Credits can be used anytime within their validity period

**Highlights**

* Unlimited hosts
* Usage-based parallel scans driven by credits
* Free 1-week re-test window
* Agentic AI Pentesting using external AI providers

**Limitations**

* No CI/CD integrations
* No issue tracking integrations
* No notification integration
* No production-safe testing
* No UI flow automation
* No custom templates
* No advanced reporting or branding

**Pricing**

* $25 per scan
* Minimum purchase: $125 (5 credits)

***

#### Professional Cloud

#### Best for: Security teams running continuous cloud-based scanning

This plan provides a subscription-based model with predictable monthly or annual pricing.

**Highlights**

* Configurable number of hosts
* One parallel scan per host
* Unlimited re-tests
* Notification integration
* Agentic AI Pentesting using customer-configured AI provider credentials
* Custom attack templates using Nuclei and Burp (standard update channel)
* Editable Word reports

**Limitations**

* No CI/CD integrations
* No issue tracking integrations
* No production-safe testing
* No static IP
* No complex UI automation

**Pricing**

* $100 per month for the first host
* $75 per month per additional host (up to 10)
* 20% discount on annual upfront payment

### Choosing the Right Plan

* Choose **Free** if you need basic, long-term access with limited reporting.
* Choose **Professional Pay-Per-Scan** for occasional or ad-hoc testing.
* Choose **Professional Cloud** for regular scanning with predictable billing.
* Choose **Enterprise Pay-Per-Scan** if you are an MSSP or consulting provider running large volumes of scans.
* Choose **Enterprise Cloud** for large teams requiring automation, governance, and compliance.
* Choose **Enterprise On-Prem** if you require private deployment and maximum operational control.

If you are unsure which plan fits your environment, our team can help assess your scanning and deployment requirements.
